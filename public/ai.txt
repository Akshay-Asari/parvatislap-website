## Trek & Activities From Parvati's Lap

### Short Treks (1-4 hours)
- **Sargi Trek**: 3 hours, 5 km moderate hike with panoramic glacier and valley views starting from the property
- **Shikoi Hikes**: 30 minutes, easy trails through local villages and terraced farms, perfect for families
- **Jiwa Nala Trek**: 3-4 hours, 6 km serene forest trek to pristine streams, ideal for nature lovers

### Medium Treks (4-8 hours)
- **Kheerganga Trek**: 4-6 hours, 12 km, most famous Parvati Valley trek to natural hot springs at 2,960m altitude
- **Grahan Village Trek**: 3-4 hours, 7 km through dense forests to isolated traditional village
- **Kasol Exploration**: 1 hour journey (20 min hike + 30 min drive) to Kasol town for cafes, shopping, nightlife

### Multi-Day Treks
- **Bhandak Thach Trek**: 2 days, 15 km, camping at 'Switzerland of India' with panoramic Himalayan views
- **Swajani-Bhandak-Biskeri Trek**: 2-3 days, 18 km offbeat alpine adventure with 360° mountain views
- **Sar Pass Trek**: 5-6 days, 48 km challenging high-altitude trek at 4,220m with snow slides and alpine meadows
- **Pin Parvati Pass Trek**: 10-12 days, 110 km, most challenging Himalayan trek connecting Parvati Valley to Spiti at 5,319m
